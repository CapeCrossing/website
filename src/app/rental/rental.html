Our Response to the Explosive Growth of Port Canaveral and the Space Coast

Cape Crossing provides the perfect opportunity for second home-buyers to enjoy a laid-back Florida lifestyle with all of the amenities of a four-star resort; and rental income potential.

Port Canaveral is fast becoming the largest vacation seaport in the world. It has experienced explosive cruise passenger growth of 14% in 2011, 21% growth in 2012, served approximately 4 million passengers in 2014, and with the addition the first of two new terminals designed to handle the new super ships. Growth of 50% to 6,000,000 is expected by 2017, and 100% growth to 8,000,000 passengers by the mid 2020s.

Cape Crossing is uniquely positioned to serve this new business opportunity with our Resort Rental Program, offering close proximity to Port Canaveral, Kennedy Space Center, world-famous Cocoa Beach, and direct access to the Orlando International Airport, as well as the numerous natural attractions in the area. Our vision is that the Cape Crossing community and lifestyle will attract guests from around the globe.

Benefits of Our Resort Rental and Management Program

Your Cape Crossing home is eligible for short-term rentals. We offer our owners the opportunity to participate in the Cape Crossing Resort Management Program and take advantage of the coming tourism growth. All property owners participating in the short-term nightly rental program with Cape Crossing Resort Management Program are eligible for Cape Crossing Resort Management services.

Owners can enjoy peace of mind knowing that our on-site team of management and marketing professionals, cleaning service, and maintenance staff is on the job 24-hours a day ensuring that their property is well taken care of, and at the same time owners receive a revenue stream from short-term nightly and weekly rentals.

We have developed a detailed and carefully mapped-out process of caring for rental properties which has evolved over the years to assure quality standards for Cape Crossing Resort owners and guests. Each component of this system has been meticulously designed to assure quality services for both owner and guest.

Onsite Management

Our on-site office will be available to accept reservations starting late summer for rental January 2016. in addition to the online reservation system. Our well-trained staff prides itself on its professional handling of inquiries and, as important, its personal concern in dealing with owner reservations and requests for information. After hours calls are handled by Cape Crossing Resort Management which can assist potential guests with reservation questions as well as contact on-call staff in case of emergencies, enabling us to help guests 24 hours a day.

Owner Representative

While all Cape Crossing Resort Management team members are able to answer questions, each owner will be assigned a dedicated Homeowner Representative as a main point of contact, and they will be able to reach them directly.
