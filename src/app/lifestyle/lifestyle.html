A Waterfront Lifestyle

Ever dreamed of affordable waterfront living?

Here's your chance.

Your Cape Crossing community offers a private marina providing a safe harbor for boats with thirty-one deep-water slips, and a boat launch to set sail at a moments notice.

Kayak, paddle board, and pontoon rentals are available for exploring the rivers, canal, or many tributaries for a relaxing afternoon paddle, or entertain friends and family while cruising at a gentle pace along the river’s edge.

Plan a day at the beach or explore the other attractions in our Merritt Island community.

There are many ways to enjoy the waterfront lifestyle at Cape Crossing, that’s why we think you’ll love it, as we do.
